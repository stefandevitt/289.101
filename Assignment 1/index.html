Introduction:

Kia Orana! Welcome to beautiful Rarotonga, a tiny island located in the archipelago of the Cook Islands, in the South Pacific. From 24th January to Feburary 4th, I was fortunate enough to explore this is island and gaze upon the beautiful nature and experience local culture.

This webpage will contain infromtation about Rarotonga, untouched landscapes in a photo album from the trip and two cultrual activities I experienced Koka Lagoon Cruise and Raro Buggy Tours. These two sections will contain information about the activities and photos of myself and my family experiencing them.

About Rarotonga:

Rarotonga is the capital island of the Cook Islands, located in the South Pacific Ocean. Avarua is the main town of Rarotonga.
