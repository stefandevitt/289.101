Introduction:

Kia Orana! Welcome to beautiful Rarotonga, a tiny island located in the archipelago of the Cook Islands, in the South Pacific. From 24th January to Feburary 4th, I was fortunate enough to explore this is island and gaze upon the beautiful nature and experience the local culture.

This webpage will contain infromtation about Rarotonga, untouched landscapes in a photo album from the trip and two cultrual activities I experienced Koka Lagoon Cruise and Raro Buggy Tours. These two sections will contain information about the activities and photos of myself and my family experiencing them.

About Rarotonga:

Location: Rarotonga is the capital island of the Cook Islands, located in the South Pacific Ocean. Avarua is the main town of Rarotonga. There are fifteen major islands in the Cook Islands, which are divided into two distinct groups of coral atolls: the Southern Cook Islands and the Northern Cook Islands. The islands were formed by volcanic activity.
